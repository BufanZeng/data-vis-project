Traffic Collision Visualization in Los Angeles County

Being one of the largest cities in the world, Los Angeles always has a considerable amount of traffic during the day despite the well-constructed road system.
Our project is designed to explore the traffic collisions data of Los Angeles county.
We visualized the dataset "Traffic Collision Data from 2010 to Present" from Los Angeles Open Data with 3 perspectives:

  • Overview: the overview of the collision amount for each year
  • Trend: the trend of the traffic collisions in daily basis
  • Demographics: some demographic information about the victims involved in the accidents
